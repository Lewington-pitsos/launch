BASIC Decription

Rock paper scissors is played between two players. Each player selects, without
Knowledge of the other player's selection, one of three alternatives:

Rock
Paper
Scissors

The players then reveal their choices, and the winner is decided by comparing
the alternatives actually chosen to a pre-decided heirachy of whuch
alternative beats which other alternatives.

Nouns: player, heirachy, selection
Verbs: select, compare
