Toctactoe is played on a 3x3 board between two players. Each player takes
A turn to occupy one of the empty squares with his/her marker. A player wins if
they ever achieve three markers in a horizontal, vertical or diagonal row.
The game ends in a tie if all board suqares are occuped and no player has won.

Nouns: board, player, square
Verbs: play, mark
